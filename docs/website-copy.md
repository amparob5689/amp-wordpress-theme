### 3.3 Client Overview

**H2:** About FloatMe

FloatMe is an earned-wage access app that provides budgeting tools, savings features, and cash advances to millions of users across the United States. As a fast-growing fintech serving financially underserved consumers, FloatMe needed a monetization strategy that generated meaningful revenue while respecting the trust their users place in the platform.

---

### 3.4 The Challenge

**H2:** The Challenge

FloatMe had an existing marketplace with a small number of advertisers, but the program was underperforming relative to its potential. Key issues included:

- **Limited partner diversity** — Only a handful of advertisers, with no systematic recruitment pipeline
- **Inconsistent quality** — Some existing partners were poorly matched to FloatMe's user base, leading to low engagement and questionable compliance
- **No strategic oversight** — The marketplace lacked dedicated management, optimization, and performance reporting
- **Untapped revenue opportunity** — With millions of engaged users, the marketplace represented a significant revenue channel that wasn't being realized

FloatMe needed a partner who understood fintech monetization, could rapidly source and vet quality advertisers, and would manage the program with the same rigor FloatMe applies to its core product.

---

### 3.5 The Solution

**H2:** What AMP Did

AMP took over full management of FloatMe's marketplace and affiliate strategy, implementing a comprehensive approach:

**Partner strategy & recruitment:**
We assessed FloatMe's user demographics and product experience to define the ideal partner profile. We then leveraged our fintech publisher network to recruit 30+ curated advertisers — each vetted for relevance, compliance, and revenue potential.

**Quality & compliance governance:**
Every advertiser was screened against financial advertising standards and FloatMe's brand guidelines. We established ongoing monitoring processes to ensure partner quality remained high as the marketplace scaled.

**Optimization & testing:**
We implemented weekly performance reviews, A/B testing of offer placements and creative assets, and data-driven commission adjustments. Underperforming partners were pruned; high performers were given expanded placement and improved terms.

**Reporting & insights:**
We delivered weekly dashboards tracking revenue, EPC, partner quality scores, and user engagement metrics — giving FloatMe's leadership team full visibility into marketplace performance and growth trajectory.

---

### 3.6 The Results

**H2:** Results

Over the course of the engagement, AMP transformed FloatMe's marketplace from an afterthought into the company's fastest-growing revenue channel:

- **+241% year-over-year revenue growth** — Affiliate marketplace revenue more than tripled, driven by higher partner volume and improved partner quality
- **+88% year-over-year EPC improvement** — Earnings per click increased significantly as we optimized placements and replaced low-performing partners with higher-converting alternatives
- **+540% active partner growth** — The marketplace expanded from a handful of advertisers to 30+ curated, brand-safe partners offering relevant financial products
- **$1M+ in marketplace revenue managed** — The affiliate channel became a material contributor to FloatMe's overall revenue

---

### 3.7 Why It Worked

**H2:** Why It Worked

FloatMe's success wasn't accidental — it came from applying fintech-specific affiliate expertise:

- **User-first curation:** We matched offers to FloatMe's audience profile (financially underserved consumers seeking savings and cash advance tools), ensuring relevance and trust
- **Compliance rigor:** Every advertiser met financial services advertising standards, protecting FloatMe's regulatory standing and brand reputation
- **Dedicated management:** The program had senior-level strategic oversight from day one — not a junior account manager learning fintech on the job
