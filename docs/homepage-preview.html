OUR PROCESS

How We Build and Scale Affiliate Programs
for Financial Brands

A proven four-phase framework refined across 14+ years of managing affiliate programs for fintech companies, banks, and financial institutions.

01

Diagnose

We audit your existing affiliate program — evaluating partner quality, tracking infrastructure, compliance gaps, and revenue opportunity. You get a clear picture of where you stand and where the upside is.

02

Design

We build a program strategy tailored to your business — commission structures, publisher targeting, compliance framework, and creative assets. Every decision grounded in what works for financial brands.

03

Launch & Optimize

We activate the program, recruit and onboard publishers, and begin optimizing in real time. Weekly performance reviews keep the program aligned with your growth targets and quality standards.

04

Scale & Govern

We expand into new partner categories, negotiate better terms, and build governance systems that protect brand integrity at scale. The goal: a channel that compounds over time.
